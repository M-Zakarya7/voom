About Voom

Voom is a coworking space and business solutions consultant that aims to assist individuals and groups in achieving their career and business goals.

This Is Our Story

We founded Voom to engage with the youth community especially those who add a value to us and our community. We bring Small start-ups, entrepreneurs and freelancers on the same space, to share contacts, establish synergies, but value also each others privacy.

We deployed the sharing economy concept to design a professional yet-economic working environment that can serve the needs of SMBs , startups , entrepreneurs and freelancers.

Who We Are.

Voom is a community of young people that learn from each other and co-create projects and ideas together.

Our Mission.

We are supporting talents, boosting culture exchange, empowering youth, building peaceful community, and We lead by example!

Our Vision.

It offers a shared workspace equipped with Wi-Fi access, as well as halls for courses, rehearsals and drills. We also have a hall equipped with birthdays.

Our Core Values.

We are Non-judgemental - we love you for who you are. We are Passionate - we are absolutely committed to supporting young people. We are Approachable - we are open minded and value diversity. We are Empowering - we enable positive change.
